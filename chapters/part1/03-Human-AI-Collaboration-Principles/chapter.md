::: {custom-style="Chapter Title"}
Human-AI Collaboration Principles
:::

Establish five principles that govern every AI-assisted modeling session throughout the book. The collaboration cheat sheet produced here is a reference the reader returns to in every chapter.

1.	The Risk Nobody Talks About — AI gets things wrong convincingly
2.	Experts First, Always — domain knowledge lives in people, not prompts
3.	Artifacts Are Your Prompts — quality of output proportional to quality of input
4.	Validate Before You Propagate — AI output entering artifacts without review becomes debt
5.	The Devil's Advocate in the Room — AI as challenger, not oracle
6.	Keeping the Human in the Loop — every Blueprint step is a human decision gate
7.	Your AI Collaboration Cheat Sheet — one-page reference
